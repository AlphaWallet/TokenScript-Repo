We need your help!

TokenScript is opensource. The site is built by a group of stewards and curators. If you contribute to it, you might be among the first to build the future of web3.

More importantly, TokenScript is an evolving standard. If you have your use case or you envision how web3 should work, this is the opportunity to shape the growth of future standards and not to let it derail from solving real world problems.
